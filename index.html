Compund exercises

Compound exercises are exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once. These are the best for muscle growth as they provide more resistance and can break down the muscle more. They also incrase your human groth hormone which will in turn increase metabolism and also build muscle. For example a bench press would significantly take precedence over the isolation exercises.

Isolation exercises include the tricep push down and the bicep curl for example. These exercises can be ok but are mostly considered average by a lot of fitnesss fanaticts. They can be beneficial but often then are just used to shape the muscle and always should be done at the end of a workout after you have used up most of your energy performing the compound exercises.
