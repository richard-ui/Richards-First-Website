CHEST

Dumbell Press

Pick up a pair of dumbells you feel you can just about lift. sit on a bench with a dumbell in each hand. Lie down and lift the dumbells above your head, pause for 1 second before lowering back dwon slowly, until dumbells are level with your head.

Dips

Find 2 parellel bars and grab. Begin to hover whilst holding onto the bars. Push yourself up and as you do this lean in slightly so you can feel the emphasis on your chest. As you get to the top lower yourself back down slowly.

BACK

Bent over row

Walk up to a barbell that is on the floor. Use just your legs to lower yourself and grab the bar. Pull the bar against your lower chest while keeping your torso horizontal. Return the bar to the floor on each rep.

Pull-up

Walk up to standard bar. Grab it with both hands with them being shoudler width apart. A true pull up should always start in the dead hang position with your arms fully extended. Initiate the pull up by squeezing the bar whilst engaging the muscles of your upper body and core. Continue to pull up until your chin reaches the bar.

LEGS

Sprinting

Accelerate as fast as you can with your legs. Remember to push off with your back foot to generate more force. As you are in full flow of sprinting, sway your arms bac and fourth. This will generate even more force allowing you to prolong the action that has been taken.

Squat

Approach a Squat rack already loaded up with a considerable amount of weight you can lift. Begin ducking under the bar and placing the barbell on your shoulders. Grab the bar with the palm of your hands and lift up. When you in the starting position lower yourself until your bottom gets to hip height.

Full body vs Splits

Full body
Both methods work can work equally aswell. A full body workout is definetely the right way to go if you are a beginner and you wan to build muscle fast. If they are done properly and consistently it would normally take around 2 months to achieve to start to see results.
The reason they are so effective as they can be performed throrughout the week without tiring out your muscles to much. These full body workout can normally be perfomed at a rate of 3 times a week. As a beginner you will develop muscle very quickly because you are just starting out and literally any weight that you pick up will make you notice results.
Splits
With splits, these are not something that should be considered by th beginner because its just not that beneficial. But for who it is beneficial for is people that have experience behind the weights already possible someone that had been working out around 6 months to a year.
There will come a time when you will start to plateau with full body workouts and therefore you will not progress any further. To outbreak this splits are great for this as it allows you to break down your muscle far more effectively than what a full body workout would bring. Also Having a weeks rest thorughout the week will allow your muscles to recover and give them the rest and time to recover.
